Extending Roots


Established in 1992, C.R. Exports has transformed over the years to become a renowned and trusted source of fresh fruits and vegetables from the pearl of the Indian Ocean. We are proudly the largest exporter of fresh fruits and vegetables in Sri Lanka and our ISO 22000 and HACCP accreditations are a fitting testament of our commitment to superior quality. We support the livelihoods of a large community of farmers both directly and indirectly, and together we strive towards extending our roots in the global perishables industry. And we haven’t stopped.. the journey for excellence continues!
